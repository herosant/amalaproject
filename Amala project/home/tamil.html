BA Tamil

The BA Tamil syllabus is designed for three years which is divided into six semesters. The program aims at providing good knowledge of the history and culture of the Tamil language. The BA Tamil course has both core and elective papers. Some of the common BA Tamil subjects are Tamil, English, Journalism, Modern Literature, etc.
